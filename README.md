# Sound-Game-Jam-2025-Navigation-of-Soltrianius
In this Catan inspired game, you are the navigator of the Celestial Cosmos Galaxy Exploration Team who is on a mission to discover the Soltrianius Galaxy, a cozy uninhabited galaxy that brings hope to many with its vast planets. You and your team hope to find a place for their civilization to be able to move to as their home planet is in danger.
